# Sales Performance & Revenue Analysis

##  Problem Statement
The objective of this case study is to analyze sales data to understand revenue trends, product performance, and regional contribution to support data-driven business decisions.

##  Key Insights
- Laptop is the highest revenue-generating product
- South region contributes the highest revenue
- Revenue dropped in February and recovered in March
- Electronics category dominates revenue and profit
- Overall profit margin is approximately 31%

## Business Recommendations
- Increase focus on high-performing products like laptops
- Strengthen marketing and operations in the South region
- Analyze the February revenue dip to identify causes
- Prioritize Electronics category for future growth
